# CaseWatch

> An open-source civic-tech platform that helps citizens of India track court hearings, navigate government certificate procedures, download official document formats, and stay protected from legal touts — with zero login required.

---

## Overview

**CaseWatch** is a bilingual (Hindi/English) legal-aid web platform built for citizens of India who lack access to reliable legal information. Anyone can look up their court case by CNR number, understand what documents they need for a government certificate, download the correct format, and verify whether a notice or legal agent is legitimate — all from a mobile browser, with no account or login needed.

The platform acts as a **trusted guide**, not a legal replacement. Every output is framed as guidance to help users take the right next step.

## Features

### Court Hearing Tracker
Enter a 16-character CNR number to instantly fetch your case details from eCourts. View your next hearing date, current case stage, bench details, and judge name. Pin multiple cases to your browser session and follow a chronological timeline of all past orders. The latest court order is summarised in plain Hindi/English powered by Gemini AI — core tracking works even if AI is unavailable.

### Government Certificate Guidance
Step-by-step guidance for obtaining government certificates across India. Each certificate page covers what the certificate is and who needs it, a checklist of documents required before you start, exact steps with official portal links, realistic processing timelines, and fraud warnings specific to that certificate type.

Certificates covered: Domicile, Caste (OBC / SC / ST), Income, Birth, Death, Character, Marriage, Residence.

> All guidance content is platform-authored. Generated drafts are labelled **"for review only"** — not ready-to-file documents.

### Downloadable Certificate Formats
Download official, standardized certificate formats with correct layout, required fields, and bilingual (Hindi/English) labels. 

### Verified Portal Directory
A curated directory of verified links to official Indian government and court portals, categorized by type: courts, certificates, legal aid, and police. Covers both central government portals (eCourts, NALSA, DigiLocker) and state-level service portals.
